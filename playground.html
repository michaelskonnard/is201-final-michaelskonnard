Tidal Ball

  1. Tidal Ball - my favorite beach game
  2. Like cornhole, but you dig a hole and trench in the sand and throw balls into them instead of bags.
    • Setup:
      • Use shovel from bag to create a hole the size of your hand
      • Use shovel to create a trench at the shovels radius away from the hole in a half circle
      • Pace 10 steps away and repeat the same for the other side
      • All ready to play!
    • How to Play:
      • Teams of two take turns throwing the red and blue balls aiming for the hole (3 points) or the trench (1 point) on the opposite side
      • After all of the balls are thrown, points are tallied and the difference in points is scored for whoever had more points
      • Play to 11 or 21
